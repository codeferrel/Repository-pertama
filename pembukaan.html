Timor-Leste,[a] also known as East Timor,[b] officially
the Democratic Republic of Timor-Leste, is a country in Southeast Asia.
It comprises the eastern half of the island of Timor, the coastal
exclave of Oecusse in the island's northwest, and the minor islands of
Atauro and Jaco.[c] Australia is the country's southern neighbour, separated
by the Timor Sea. The country's size is 14,950 square kilometres
(5,770 sq mi). Dili, on the north coast of Timor, is its capital and largest city.
